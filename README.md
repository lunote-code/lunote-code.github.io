## 🧠 Turn notes into a system

Most note apps store information.

**Lunote helps you build a thinking system.**

Instead of static notes, you get a workspace that adapts to your workflow.

---

## 🔌 Build your own workspace with plugins

Lunote is designed around a plugin-driven architecture.

You can:
- Extend functionality with plugins
- Customize your workflow
- Shape your own productivity system
- Turn notes into structured knowledge

---

## ⚡ Why Lunote

- Lightweight and fast
- Minimal and distraction-free
- Extensible by design
- Built for developers, writers, and thinkers
